FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment to visit I-Smile Dental Clinic?▼
No appointment is necessary — walk-ins are always welcome at both our Gbagada branches! However, booking in advance ensures you get your preferred time slot. You can call us on 08133557279 or fill out the form above.
How much does teeth whitening cost at I-Smile?▼
We offer very affordable whitening packages. The exact price depends on your specific needs and treatment type. Please call us on 08133557279 or WhatsApp us for current pricing — we'll be happy to discuss the best option for your budget.
Are your dental treatments suitable for children?▼
Absolutely! We love treating children. Our team is trained in pediatric dentistry and we create a fun, gentle, fear-free experience for kids of all ages. We recommend starting dental check-ups as early as age 2.
Which branch should I go to?▼
Both branches offer the same full range of services. Choose whichever is most convenient for you — Branch 1 is at Orile Pedeoro Compound Pedro, and Branch 2 is on Church Street, Gbagada. Both are open Monday to Saturday, 8am–6pm.
Is root canal treatment painful at I-Smile?▼
Not at all! Modern root canal treatment at I-Smile is virtually painless. We use local anaesthesia and modern techniques to ensure you are completely comfortable throughout the procedure. Most patients are surprised at how easy it is.
How do I know which treatment I need?▼
Just come in for a check-up! Our experienced dentists will thoroughly examine your teeth and gums, listen to your concerns and goals, and recommend the best treatment plan for you — with no pressure. First consultations are very affordable.
