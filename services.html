Dental Implants
Restorative

Dental Implants

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. A titanium post is placed into the jawbone, acting as an artificial tooth root, topped with a natural-looking crown.

  • Permanent, lifetime tooth replacement
  • Prevents bone loss and face shape change
  • Functions exactly like natural teeth
  • Easy to clean and maintain

Root Canal Treatment

At I-Smile, root canal therapy is comfortable and stress-free. We use modern techniques and local anaesthesia to eliminate pain and save your natural tooth from extraction.

  • Pain-free procedure with local anaesthesia
  • Saves your natural tooth
  • Eliminates infection and prevents spread
  • Completed in as few as 1–2 visits

Crowns & Bridges

Crowns & Bridges

Dental crowns restore damaged, cracked, or weakened teeth to full function and appearance. Bridges fill gaps left by missing teeth, giving you a complete, beautiful smile.

  • Porcelain, ceramic, and metal-ceramic options
  • Custom-made for a perfect fit
  • Lasts 10–15+ years with proper care
  • Fixed bridges for missing teeth
