Who We Are

Gbagada's Trusted Dental Home

I-Smile Dental Clinic was founded with one mission: to make world-class dental care accessible, affordable, and comfortable for every family in Gbagada, Lagos.

We believe that a healthy smile transforms not just your appearance but your confidence and quality of life. That's why we combine cutting-edge dental technology with a warm, family-oriented approach that makes every patient feel at home.

With two conveniently located branches in Gbagada and a team of experienced, compassionate dental professionals, we're right here in your neighbourhood, ready to serve you.

To provide accessible, affordable, and expert dental care that improves the oral health and overall wellbeing of every patient and family in Gbagada, Lagos.

To be the most trusted and beloved dental clinic in Lagos — where every patient walks out with a brighter smile and greater confidence in their health.

Our Journey

Growing With Gbagada

2018

Founded

I-Smile Dental Clinic opens its first branch at Orile Pedeoro Compound, Pedro, Gbagada — bringing expert dental care to the heart of the community.

2020

Expanding Services

We add advanced cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and pediatric dentistry to our service range, responding to growing community demand.

2022

Second Branch Opens

Branch 2 opens on Church Street, Gbagada — making I-Smile even more accessible to the entire neighbourhood.

Today

Serving Gbagada & Beyond

Two thriving branches, a growing team of dental professionals, and hundreds of happy patients — Gbagada's most trusted dental home.
